
Section 04: Variables Scope and Illustration
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Variables Scopes I

	- Variables have scopes

	- A variable scope refers to the accessibility
	  of a variable in a given program or function
	
	- For example, a variable may only be available
	  within a specific function, or it may be 
	  available to the entire C program
